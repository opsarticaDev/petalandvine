Consignment

We accept high-quality, pre-owned luxury items including designer handbags, fine jewelry, women's clothing (current styles within 3 years), designer shoes, and select home décor. All items must be authentic, clean, and in excellent condition. We're selective to maintain the curated quality our clients expect. Please review our full Consignment Agreement for detailed acceptance criteria.
Your commission depends on the final selling price. Items selling under $100 earn 40%, $100-$499 earns 50%, $500-$999 earns 55%, $1,000-$4,999 earns 60%, and items over $5,000 earn 65%. We price items competitively based on current market conditions to help your pieces sell quickly.
Our standard consignment period is 90 days. After 45 days, prices may be reduced by up to 25% to encourage sale. At the end of the period, unsold items must be retrieved within 14 days. You may also request to extend the consignment period, subject to our approval.
Payments are processed on the 15th of each month for items sold during the previous month. We offer payment by check, direct deposit, or store credit (store credit includes a 5% bonus!). The minimum payout threshold is $25—smaller amounts roll over to the next pay period.
Getting started is easy! Email photos and descriptions of your items to consign@petalandvine.com. We'll review and respond within 3 business days. If we're interested, we'll schedule an appointment for you to bring items in. You'll complete our consignment paperwork, receive a consignment number, and we'll handle the rest!
We'll notify you when your consignment period ends. You have 14 days to pick up unsold items or request an extension. Items not retrieved within 14 days become property of Petal & Vine and may be donated to charity. We recommend responding promptly to our notifications!
