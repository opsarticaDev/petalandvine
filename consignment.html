Consignment Agreement

Last Updated: January 2024

Welcome to Our Consignment Program

Thank you for considering Petal & Vine as your consignment partner. We are dedicated to finding the perfect new home for your luxury items while ensuring you receive fair compensation for your treasures. This agreement outlines the terms and conditions of our consignment relationship.

By consigning items with Petal & Vine, you ("Consignor") agree to all terms outlined in this agreement. Please read carefully before submitting items for consignment.

What We Accept

Petal & Vine specializes in high-quality, pre-owned luxury items. We accept:

  • Designer Handbags & Accessories: Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Hermès, Gucci, Prada, and comparable brands
  • Fine Jewelry: Designer and precious metal pieces in excellent condition
  • Women's Clothing: Designer and contemporary brands, current styles (within 3 years)
  • Shoes: Designer footwear with minimal wear
  • Home Décor: Select luxury decorative items

Acceptance Criteria

All items must be:

  • Authentic (we reserve the right to authenticate all items)
  • Clean and in excellent to like-new condition
  • Free of significant damage, stains, odors, or excessive wear
  • Current in style (generally within 3-5 years for clothing)
  • Appropriate for our clientele and brand aesthetic

Commission Structure

Our commission rates are based on the final selling price of your item:

Selling Price Your Share Petal & Vine
Under $100 40% 60%
$100 - $499 50% 50%
$500 - $999 55% 45%
$1,000 - $4,999 60% 40%
$5,000+ 65% 35%

Consignment Period

The standard consignment period is 90 days from the date items are accepted and priced. During this period:

  • Items will be displayed in our boutique and/or on our online platforms
  • Pricing is determined by Petal & Vine based on market conditions, brand, condition, and demand
  • Price reductions of up to 25% may occur after 45 days to encourage sale
  • Consignors may request a specific minimum price, which may affect acceptance

Payment Terms

Upon sale of your item(s):

  • Payments are processed on the 15th of each month for items sold in the previous month
  • Minimum payout threshold: $25 (smaller amounts roll over to next period)
  • Payment methods: Check, direct deposit, or store credit (store credit receives 5% bonus)
  • Consignors are responsible for any applicable taxes on their earnings

Unsold Items

If items do not sell during the consignment period:

  • Consignors will be notified via email/phone when the consignment period ends
  • Items must be retrieved within 14 days of notification
  • Consignors may choose to extend the consignment period (subject to approval)
  • Unclaimed items after 14 days become property of Petal & Vine and may be donated to charity or disposed of at our discretion

Consignor Responsibilities

By consigning with us, you agree that:

  • You are the legal owner of all consigned items or authorized to sell them
  • All items are authentic and legally obtained
  • Items are accurately described and free of undisclosed defects
  • You will provide valid identification and contact information
  • You will respond to communications within 7 days

Petal & Vine Responsibilities

We commit to:

  • Handle your items with care and store them in a secure environment
  • Market and display items professionally
  • Maintain accurate records of all transactions
  • Process payments in a timely manner
  • Communicate promptly regarding sales, price changes, or issues

Loss, Damage & Liability

While we take every precaution to protect your items:

  • Petal & Vine is not responsible for loss or damage due to theft, fire, flood, or other unforeseen circumstances
  • Our liability is limited to 50% of the agreed-upon selling price
  • We recommend consignors maintain their own insurance for high-value items
  • Items are not insured while in our possession unless separately arranged

Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement:

  • Consignors may withdraw items at any time with 7 days written notice
  • Petal & Vine may return items at any time if they no longer meet our standards
  • Termination does not affect items already sold or pending transactions

How to Consign

Ready to consign with us? Here's how to get started:

  1. Submit Photos: Email photos and descriptions of items to consign@petalandvine.com
  2. Review: We'll respond within 3 business days with items we'd like to see in person
  3. Appointment: Schedule a drop-off appointment at our boutique
  4. Agreement: Complete consignment paperwork and receive your consignment number
  5. Get Paid: Receive payment when your items sell!
